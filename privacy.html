Privacy Policy

Introduction

LA Hood Cleaning ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, and interact with us.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services. By accessing and using LA Hood Cleaning's website and services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the provisions of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Business information (restaurant name, business type, location)
  • Service request details and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Communication history and inquiries
  • Feedback, reviews, and testimonials

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity:

  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website or application
  • Click patterns and interaction data
  • Geographic location (approximate)

Third-Party Information

We may receive information about you from third parties, including business partners, service providers, and publicly available sources. This information helps us verify details you've provided and enhance the accuracy of our records.

How We Use Your Information

LA Hood Cleaning uses the information we collect for various purposes, including:

Service Delivery

To provide, maintain, and improve our hood cleaning services, schedule appointments, send service reminders, and communicate with you about your service requests.

Communication

To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, send transactional emails, and keep you informed about service updates and compliance documentation.

Marketing and Promotional

To send promotional materials, newsletters, and updates about special offers (with your consent). You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

Business Operations

To process payments, maintain records, manage accounts, generate invoices, and maintain compliance documentation for fire code requirements.

Analytics and Improvement

To analyze website usage patterns, understand customer preferences, improve our services, and optimize our marketing efforts.

Legal Compliance

To comply with legal obligations, enforce our agreements, protect our rights, and prevent fraud or other illegal activities.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website and collect information about your browsing activity.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly, including session management and security features.

Analytical Cookies

We use Google Analytics and similar tools to understand how visitors interact with our website, measure performance, and identify areas for improvement.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide a more personalized experience on subsequent visits.

Marketing Cookies

We use marketing cookies to track the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns and to display relevant content to you.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive notification when a cookie is being set

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to access certain features.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, sending communications, and delivering services.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government request, or when necessary to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

Business Transfers

If LA Hood Cleaning is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Consent

We may share your information with other parties when you have given us explicit consent to do so.

Data Security

LA Hood Cleaning implements comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password storage using industry-standard practices
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure data storage with appropriate access controls
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Right to Correction

You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information in our records.

Right to Deletion

You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal and operational exceptions.

Right to Opt-Out

You can opt-out of marketing communications and certain data processing activities by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.

Right to Data Portability

You may request that we provide your information in a portable format that can be transferred to another service provider.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites or services, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites before providing your information or engaging with their services.

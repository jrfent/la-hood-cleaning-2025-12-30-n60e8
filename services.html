Our Professional Services

Comprehensive hood cleaning and kitchen exhaust solutions designed to keep your restaurant compliant, safe, and operating at peak efficiency.

What We Offer

From routine maintenance to emergency cleanings, we provide a full range of hood cleaning and kitchen exhaust services tailored to your business needs.

Hood Cleaning

Professional NFPA96 compliant hood cleaning services that remove grease buildup and ensure optimal ventilation. We provide detailed documentation for fire code compliance and can schedule same-day service when needed.

Grease Trap Cleaning

Regular grease trap maintenance prevents costly plumbing issues and environmental violations. Our certified technicians pump, clean, and dispose of grease properly in compliance with all local regulations.

Ductwork Cleaning

Deep cleaning of exhaust ductwork removes accumulated grease and improves air quality. Clean ducts enhance ventilation efficiency, reduce fire risk, and extend the lifespan of your HVAC system.

Fire Safety Inspections

Comprehensive fire safety assessments of your kitchen exhaust system to identify potential hazards. We provide detailed reports and recommendations to ensure your facility meets all fire code requirements.

Emergency Cleaning

Got a grease fire or sudden ventilation failure? Our emergency response team is available 24/7 to handle urgent hood cleaning and system restoration to get your kitchen back online quickly.

Maintenance Plans

Customized maintenance schedules keep your hood system in peak condition year-round. Choose monthly, quarterly, or semi-annual service plans with priority scheduling and discounted rates.

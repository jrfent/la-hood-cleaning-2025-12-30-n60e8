The Complete Guide to NFPA96 Hood Cleaning Compliance for Los Angeles Restaurants

LA Hood Cleaning's comprehensive guide explains why NFPA96 compliance is critical for Los Angeles restaurants. The National Fire Protection Association Standard 96 establishes mandatory hood cleaning schedules—monthly, quarterly, or semi-annually—depending on cooking volume and grease production. Professional hood cleaning removes dangerous grease buildup from canopies, ductwork, fans, and roof penetrations, preventing fires and avoiding costly violations. Non-compliance risks substantial fines, operational shutdowns, and liability exposure. The guide emphasizes choosing certified professionals who provide proper documentation, implementing complementary safety measures, and maintaining detailed compliance records. Taking proactive action protects your business, staff, and customers from fire hazards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hood cleaning services and fire code compliance.

How often should I get my hood cleaned?

The frequency depends on your cooking volume and type of cooking. NFPA96 standards require cleaning based on your specific operation: high-volume cooking with heavy grease may require monthly cleaning, while lower-volume operations might need quarterly service. We'll assess your kitchen and recommend the optimal schedule.

Can you clean my hood while we're still operating?

Yes. We specialize in minimal-disruption cleaning and can work around your operating schedule. Many clients book early mornings, late nights, or slower weekday periods so service doesn't affect their kitchen.

What happens if I fail a fire inspection?

Don't panic. We offer emergency hood cleaning specifically for this situation. We'll prioritize your location for same-day or next-day service, bring your system into full compliance, and provide all documentation the fire marshal requires.

Do you provide documentation for compliance?

Absolutely. Every service comes with a comprehensive report, photos, and a certificate of NFPA96 compliance. Perfect for inspections, audits, and insurance.

What areas of Los Angeles do you service?

We proudly serve all of Los Angeles and surrounding areas including Long Beach, Pasadena, Santa Monica, and beyond. Based in South LA, we can typically reach any location in the greater LA area within 24 hours for emergency service.

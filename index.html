About LA Hood Cleaning

Founded in 2015, LA Hood Cleaning began with a simple mission: to help Los Angeles restaurants maintain safe, compliant kitchen exhaust systems. Our founder, with over 20 years of experience in commercial kitchen maintenance, recognized a critical gap in the market for reliable, professional hood cleaning services. What started as a small operation has grown into the most trusted hood cleaning company serving over 500 restaurants across the Los Angeles area.

We believe that every restaurant deserves access to professional, certified hood cleaning services that prioritize safety, compliance, and minimal disruption to business operations. Our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer-first service guide every decision we make. We're not just cleaning hoods—we're protecting businesses, preventing fires, and giving restaurant owners the peace of mind they deserve. Every member of our team is certified, insured, and committed to delivering exceptional results on every single job.
